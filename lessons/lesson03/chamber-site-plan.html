Chamber of Commerce - Sandton, Johannesburg

Site Name

The site is named "Sandton Chamber of Commerce" to reflect the locale (Sandton) and its purpose (Chamber of Commerce).

Site Purpose

The Sandton Chamber of Commerce website serves the following purposes:

  1. Promote local businesses and economic development in Sandton.
  2. Provide information about the Sandton community, events, and attractions.

Scenarios

  1. What events will the chamber be holding this month that promote business-to-business networking?
  2. Where can I find contact information for the chamber's board of directors?
  3. What has been the population growth in the Sandton area?
